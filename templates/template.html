Examples

You can use the following commands to get your visitor information in different formats:

Plain Text (Linux/macOS):

curl -H "Accept: text/plain" https://ip.sbro.nl/

JSON (Linux/macOS):

curl -H "Accept: application/json" https://ip.sbro.nl/

PowerShell (Windows):

Invoke-WebRequest -Headers @{Accept='application/json'} https://ip.sbro.nl/ | Select-Object -ExpandProperty Content
